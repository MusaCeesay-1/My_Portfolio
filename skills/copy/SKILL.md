# Copy

## Objective

Improve page copy without changing underlying meaning.

## Workflow

1. Identify audience and intent for the section.
2. Keep key facts intact and remove filler.
3. Improve clarity, hierarchy, and readability.
4. Align tone with professional portfolio positioning.
5. Provide final copy ready to paste into HTML.

## Output Rules

- Keep statements specific and evidence-based.
- Avoid hype, jargon, or generic claims.
- Prefer short paragraphs and clear headings.
- Maintain consistency across sections.
