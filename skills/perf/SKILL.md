# Perf

## Objective

Improve perceived and measured page performance.

## Workflow

1. Identify major payload and render bottlenecks.
2. Prioritize high-impact, low-risk optimizations first.
3. Apply changes to assets, markup, and styles as needed.
4. Re-check page behavior to avoid visual or functional regressions.
5. Report before/after impact and remaining opportunities.

## Output Rules

- Focus on measurable wins.
- Avoid premature micro-optimizations.
- Keep readability and maintainability intact.
- Do not sacrifice accessibility for speed.
